# PRD — Resilinc Lite (Hackathon MVP)

## Product Goal
Build a simple, deployable supply chain risk intelligence SaaS that helps operations teams detect disruptions, prioritize supplier risk, and coordinate incident response.

## Target Users
- Risk managers
- Procurement leads
- Ops leaders

## MVP Features
1. Authenticated org workspace.
2. Supplier/facility/part registry.
3. Risk event ingestion and normalization.
4. Supplier risk scoring + alert generation.
5. Incident action tracking board.
6. Seed data for immediate demo readiness.

## Non-Goals (Hackathon Timebox)
- Deep ERP integrations.
- Full digital twin simulation.
- Complex ML pipelines.

## Success Criteria
- Core user journey works end-to-end in deployed environment.
- Mobile-compatible dashboard with meaningful seeded data.
- Secure schema with RLS on all tables.
