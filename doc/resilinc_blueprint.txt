Executive Summary
Supply Chain Risk Management platforms help organizations monitor, assess, and mitigate disruptions across their entire
supply network through real-time visibility and predictive analytics. With global supply chains becoming increasingly complex
and vulnerable to disruptions like natural disasters, geopolitical events, and pandemics, companies need intelligent systems to
proactively identify risks and maintain business continuity. The market opportunity is massive as enterprises seek to build
resilient, adaptive supply chains that can withstand modern challenges.

MVP Scope
The MVP should include core supplier risk monitoring, basic disruption alerts, simple supply chain mapping, incident response
workflows, and essential integrations with common ERP systems. Focus on delivering immediate value through real-time risk
visibility and actionable alerts for the most common disruption scenarios.


Competitive Landscape
The supply chain risk management space includes established players like Resilinc, as well as broader supply chain visibility
platforms and traditional risk management tools. Most existing solutions focus on monitoring and alerting, with limited predictive
capabilities and automation. There's significant opportunity for innovation in AI-driven prediction, automated response, and
collaborative intelligence sharing.
